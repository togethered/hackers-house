> What do we mean by a "hacker house"?

By a hacker house, we mean the original definition of a hacker space. Not this current era of AI-crazed YCombinator frat hacker houses.

Things like 3D printer, homelab, hack nights, movie nights, cooking nights, music events, writing parties.


Groups that capture this spirit are the Bug Apartment, which self describes as a "nonbinary frat house" and [Playspace](https://jzhao.xyz/thoughts/Playspace), captured by the epithet "secular maker church".
